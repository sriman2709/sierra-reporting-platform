# Public Transparency Portal

The Public Transparency Portal is a **citizen-facing, no-login-required** view of the organization's financial data. It is accessible at `/transparency` without any authentication.

## Access

**URL:** [public-sector-reporting.azurewebsites.net/transparency](https://public-sector-reporting.azurewebsites.net/transparency)

No account or login is required. Share this URL freely with citizens, media, oversight bodies, and stakeholders.

---

## What Citizens Can See

### Hero KPIs
6 headline metrics displayed prominently:

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Active Grants | Number of active grant awards |
| Total Grant Funding | Total awarded grant dollars |
| Total Spending YTD | All government spending year-to-date |
| Total Revenue YTD | All revenue collected year-to-date |
| Cash Reserves | Current liquid cash position |
| Tax Revenue YTD | Tax collections year-to-date |

### Tabs

#### Overview
Summary narrative and KPI highlights — an at-a-glance financial health view.

#### Grant Awards
Complete list of all grants received by the organization:

- Grant number, program name, funding agency, award amount, start/end date, status
- **Search box** — filter the grant table in real time by any field
- Sortable by amount, agency, or status

#### Fund Spending
How government money is being spent by fund:

- Fund name, fund type, budget, YTD expenditure, encumbrances, available balance, burn %
- Pie chart: spending distribution by fund type

#### Tax Revenue
Tax collection performance:

- Property Tax, Sales Tax, Utility Tax, Hotel/Motel Tax, Business License Tax
- Collected vs budgeted bar chart per tax type
- Monthly trend line for the fiscal year

#### Program Outcomes
Public program performance metrics:

- Program name, metric name, target, actual, achievement %
- Green checkmarks for on-track metrics, red flags for off-track
- Communicates to citizens what their tax dollars are achieving

#### CAFR Summary
Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR) style summary by fund type:

- General Fund / Special Revenue / Capital Projects / Debt Service / Enterprise
- Total assets, liabilities, fund balance, net position
- Totals row at the bottom for the full government-wide picture

---

## Data Disclaimer

All data is sourced from the organization's SAP HANA Cloud financial management system. Financial data is updated based on period-close cycles. Program outcome data is updated as reported by program managers. This portal is provided for public transparency and does not constitute an official audited financial statement.

---

## For Staff

Internal staff can access the public portal via the **Public Portal** link in the sidebar under "Phase 5 · Public Transparency." This opens the same public view, useful for previewing what citizens see or sharing with stakeholders during meetings.
