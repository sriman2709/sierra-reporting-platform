# Financial Forecast

Build what-if scenarios, run fund sensitivity analysis, and monitor budget variance across the fiscal year.

## Overview

Connects to `I_Fund` and `I_BudgetLine` to support multi-scenario financial planning.

---

## Tabs

### Variance Dashboard
Current period budget vs actuals variance for all funds:

- Funds with >10% unfavorable variance highlighted
- Trend line showing variance movement over the fiscal year
- Drill down to department level

### What-If Scenarios
Model the financial impact of hypothetical changes:

| Scenario | Description |
|---|---|
| Revenue Reduction | % reduction in anticipated revenue |
| Expenditure Increase | % increase in department budgets |
| Grant Loss | Removal of a specific grant from the portfolio |
| Interest Rate Change | Impact on investment income and variable-rate debt |

Adjust sliders and see the projected impact on fund balances and available-to-spend in real time.

### Sensitivity Analysis
Fund resilience analysis — how sensitive is each fund's balance to changes in key variables:

- Revenue sensitivity (how much can revenue drop before the fund goes negative)
- Expenditure sensitivity (how much can spending increase before over-budget)
- Results displayed as a heat map: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW sensitivity

- *"Run a sensitivity analysis on all funds."*
- *"What happens to our General Fund balance if revenue drops 10%?"*
- *"Which funds are most sensitive to expenditure increases?"*
