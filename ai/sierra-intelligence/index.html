    Sierra Intelligence — AI Analyst¶

    Sierra Intelligence is a conversational AI analyst with live access to all 14 platform modules simultaneously. Ask any question in plain English and receive a data-backed answer with an optional chart.

    How It Works¶

    1. You type a question
    2. Sierra AI determines which of its 46 data tools to call
    3. It executes those tools in parallel against live SAP HANA Cloud data
    4. It synthesizes the results into a concise answer with specific numbers
    5. It returns a chart when comparison or trend data adds value
    6. It surfaces 3 follow-up suggestions for deeper exploration

    Follow-Up Suggestions¶

    After every answer, Sierra Intelligence proposes 3 context-aware follow-up questions based on what was just returned. These appear as clickable blue buttons below the answer bubble.

    Why this matters: The follow-ups are generated by the AI based on the specific data in the answer — not generic prompts. They guide you to drill down, explore a related domain, or take a remediation action.

    Example flow: 

    You:   "Which grants are over-burning?"
AI:    [Lists 3 over-burning grants with burn rates]
AI suggests:
  › What is the compliance posture of these over-burning grants?
  › Which subrecipients are associated with these grants?
  › What corrective actions should we take to reduce burn rate?

    Click any suggestion to fire it immediately. The new answer will then generate fresh follow-ups.

    What Sierra AI Can Answer¶

    Financial Management¶

    • Grant portfolio status, burn rate, compliance, subrecipient risk
    • Fund balances, available-to-spend, over-budget alerts
    • Budget vs actuals by department
    • Contract expiry, vendor risk, AP aging
    • Cash position, investments, debt service, tax revenue
    • Forecast scenarios and sensitivity analysis

    Program & Compliance¶

    • Program effectiveness scores and trends
    • Cost-per-outcome vs peer benchmarks
    • Grant–outcome linkage and federal performance reporting
    • Audit findings, evidence completeness, subrecipient monitoring

    Enterprise Operations¶

    • Capital project health and milestone status
    • Asset condition and work order backlog
    • Inventory stock levels and reorder alerts
    • PM compliance and failure analysis

    Workforce & Fleet¶

    • Headcount, vacancy rate, position control
    • Payroll allocation by fund (grant-funded FTEs)
    • Vehicle health, inspection compliance, fuel cost
    • Fleet operating cost by department

    Cross-Domain¶

    • "What is our biggest operational risk this month?" — calls assets + capital + inventory + fleet + finance
    • "Show me the full cost picture for Public Works" — budget + assets + work orders + inventory + fleet + HR
    • "How much are we spending on grant-funded staff?" — HR payroll + grants KPIs

    Chart Types¶

    Sierra Intelligence returns charts when they add analytical value:

    Chart Type When Used
    Bar Comparing values across grants, departments, vendors, funds (≤15 items)
    Line Time-series trends — actuals over fiscal months/periods
    Pie Part-of-whole breakdowns — risk tiers, status distribution (≤6 slices)
    None Regulatory lookups, yes/no answers, single-value responses

    Tips for Best Results¶

    • Be specific: "Which grants are expiring in the next 30 days?" gives better results than "Tell me about grants."
    • Use numbers: "Show me the top 5 over-budget departments" — the AI respects quantity constraints.
    • Cross-domain questions work well: The 46 tools mean Sierra AI can join data across modules that you'd normally view separately.
    • Use follow-ups: The follow-up suggestions are tuned to the data just returned — they often uncover insights you wouldn't have thought to ask.

    Opening Prompts¶

    Sierra Intelligence shows 8 randomly rotated suggested prompts from a bank of 18 covering all domains. After your first question, follow-up suggestions take over to guide the conversation.

    Limitations¶

    • Sierra Intelligence can only access data that exists in SAP HANA Cloud via the platform's 46 tools. It cannot access external data sources.
    • Very broad questions ("Tell me everything about the platform") may produce answers covering only the most relevant domains.
    • Answers are grounded in live data — Sierra AI will say "data unavailable" if a tool returns no results rather than hallucinating values.
